Patagonia

After 2 years on the couch, your friend has asked you to start paying rent. He no longer considers you a house guest. Trim season is over and you're bored with working on the mountain. It's time to set your sights south and grab a ticket to this Argentinian wilderness and hiker's paradise. Bear Grylls has nothing on you.

the Bahamas

Tired of visiting tiki bars and Chinese restaurants for your rum cocktails? The beach bum in you is bursting to get out and sink your feet into the sand. Book a trip to this island paradise and enjoy high times in the tropics. Throw on some Grateful Dead and lay back and sip a Mai Tai as you watch the sun roll by.

the Mayan Riviera

You fancy yourself a bit of an Indiana Jones, don't you? Relive your fantasies while visiting some of the many Mayan ruins scattered through this southern Mexico Peninsula or swimming in an ancient Cenote. You're not here to party in Cancun but travel through the jungle, maybe come face to face with a Jaguar.

a Cruise Ship

Now is your chance to live out your own episode of Love Boat. It's time to put your shuffle board and line dancing skills to the test. A week at sea will be the perfect thing for those aching bones. Don't forget to pack your Hawaiian shirt.

France

You travelled through France in your early twenties, on a backpacking adventure through Europe. You thought you were going to be the next Baudelaire. You've since exchanged your army surplus gear for Louis Vuitton. Induldge in fine wines, fine foods, and fashion with your trip to this luxurious country. Isn't there a saying, "When in France..."?

Ireland

The Emereld Isle is the history buff's paradise. Enjoy a dram of whiskey at a pub older then the United States, and listen to a fiddler play his jig. Would you rather visit a whiskey mill or the Guiness Brewery today? Afterwards its off to Foynes, home of the Irish Coffee. The finer points of history begin to blur for you along with your days. That was the Blarney Stone you kissed wasn't it?

Macau

You made a fortune selling your first tech start-up and now you have some cash to burn at the tables. Vegas ain't hip anymore, unless your on your way out to Coachella. Macau is where its at. Brush shoulders with the asian elite at some of the most opulant hotels you could imagine. Enjoy one of the fastest growing metropolitan areas in the world and a culture different from anywhere else.

Salt Lake City

Bask in the wholesome glory of Salt Lake City. Your among the chosen here. What's not to love about temples, salt lakes, and fine morals. No need to experience exotic foods or to meet diverse people when you've got the Morman Tabernacle Choir.

Switzerland

You've got some cash to hide but too worried about the state of affairs in the offshore world? Switzerland is your tried and true option. Hit the slopes while you wait for the paperwork to clear. Rest easy, you can now rent your favorite Chalet using BitCoin. Go into town and buy some fine chocolates, enjoy them by the fire. You've had a busy day.

Jam Cruise

  • Exotic locals
  • Blazing guitar solos.
  • A few hundred of your closest friends.
