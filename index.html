Patagonia

You've already hiked the Appalachian Trail and the Sierra Crest. Bagged a few 14000 fters. Your pack only weighs 3.5lbs. It's time to book your passage to the end of the earth.

Bahamas

Take it slow while sipping a fruity cocktail. Leave your shoes at home, you won't need them in the land of sun. Don't forget your sunscreen though.

Mayan Riveria

You have an adventurous spirit but also wonder at ancient culture and beliefs. You dream of being the next Indiana Jones. Come down to the Mayan Riveria where you can tour ruins, snorkle through Cenotes, and four wheel around the beach. Whatever your mind can imagine I am sure you will find it here.

France

Why not stay drunk on wine for a week straight, who's counting? Isn't there a saying, "When in france..." You're a little more romantic after a bottle of the old vino. You're set for some decadent meals and an idyllc country-side as background. You've done the backpacking through Europe thing. Now your only bag is a Vutton.

Ireland

Tour some old castles and here the tails of the centuries handed down. Maybe you'll try to gain the gift of gab and kiss the ol' Blarney Stone. If that doesn't get you talking about the history of the Emerald Isle, perhaps a sip of the fine Irish Whiskey at the corner pub will get the tongue working. Or at least a bit disinfected after you stood in a line of hundreds to kiss the same dirty stone. Listen to a fiddle play a reel and drift off through the ages to an Irish Lullaby.

Macau

You made a fortune selling your first tech start-up and now you have some cash to burn at the tables. Vegas ain't hip anymore, unless your on your way out to Coachella. Macau is where its at. Brush soliders with the asian elite. Stay in some of the most opulant hotels in the world. You're not the stop and smell the roses type of person.

Salt Lake City

Walk the lovely clean streets of Salt Lake City and enjoy some good wholesome all American good times. Here those choirs sing. Who says you're boring

Switzerland

Hit the slopes! The alps is home to some of the finest winter sports in the world. Stay in a posh chalet and ski up to your door. While your at it indulge a bit in the fine chocolate. Maybe open a bank account or buy a knew pocket knife?

Easter Islands

The Easter Islands are a great destination to get away from it all. Beautiful shores, few inhabitants, and a culture and history unlike anywhere else.

Jam Cruise

  • Exotic locals,
  • Blazing guitar solos,
  • A few hundred of your closest friends,

All this and more awaits you on Jam Cruise. Do you suffer from too much FOMO about missing a festival to take a real vacation? Do you use terms like FOMO? Well Jam Cruise is the perfect option for you. You can cruise the blue waters of the Carribbean while jamming to your favorite bands. While you may be far too hung over from the late night set to make it to the shore, at least you can say you saw Fruition jam with Greensky Bluegrass while snorkling with manatees.
